## Definition List

- **Allergen:** A substance present in food that causes an allergic reaction in some people.  

- **Regulation:** An official rule made by a government or authority to control and ensure companies follow required standards.  

- **Allergen Declaration:** A rule requiring food manufacturers to clearly mention allergens present in a product on the label.  

- **Source Declaration:** Mentioning the origin or source of ingredients used in a product.  

- **Skimmed Milk:** Milk from which most or all of the fat content has been removed.  

- **Toned Milk:** A type of milk made in India by mixing buffalo milk with skimmed milk, skimmed milk powder, and water to reduce fat content and maintain non-fat solids.  

- **Food Additives:** Substances added to food to improve taste, appearance, texture, or preservation.

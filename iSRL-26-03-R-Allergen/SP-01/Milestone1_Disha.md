# 1 Definitions
- Allergen - A substance present in food that causes an allergic reaction in some people.

- Regulation - An official rule that is made by a government or authority to control and make sure that the companies must follow the issued rules.

- Allergen Declaration - A rule made for food manufacturers containing a kind of product that can cause allergies to strictly mention allergens on the product label.

- Source Declaration - It means mentioning the origin or source of the product.

## Concept and terms i need to understand 
### 1. Epidemiological Data 
It is a data that measures how often the diseases have affected the population 
### 2. Food allergy 
Condition that immune system acts abnormal to certain food causing rashes, swelling etc..
### 3. Allergen
The substance that triggers the allergy
### 4. Population burden 
How common the condition is among the population and how many are affected
### 5. FSSAI mandatory declaration list
The list of the allergen given to the manufacturers by FSSAI that need to be declare in their products to inform customers 
### 6. Shellfish allergy
Allergy caused by eating crab, lobster, prawn etc..
### 7. Digital Object Identifier
Identifier given to acedemic articles
### 8. Prevalence
People who has a certain condition at specific time

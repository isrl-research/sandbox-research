## Definition List 
**1.IgE-mediated Allergy** -> Allergic reaction triggered by immunoglobulin E antibodies , causes mast cell activation  
**2.Sensitisation** -> The presence of allergen-specific IgE antibodies which may correspond to clinical allergy  
**3.Meta-analysis** -> Collecting result from many different studies in same topic and grouping then to fine overall conclusion
